History

The Rafting Company was founded over twenty years ago by a team of outdoor enthusiasts who shared a passion for adventure, teamwork, and the beauty of the river. What began as a small family business offering local guided trips has grown into a trusted outfitter known for safety, expertise, and exceptional guest experiences. Our certified guides have led thousands of adventurers through breathtaking canyons and exhilarating rapids, all while maintaining a deep commitment to preserving the natural environments we explore. At The Rafting Company, we believe that every trip is more than just a ride down the river—it's an opportunity to connect with nature, build lasting memories, and discover the thrill of the wild.
